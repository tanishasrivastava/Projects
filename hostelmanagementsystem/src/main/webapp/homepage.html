Welcome to the Campus Comfort Inns !

The Hostel accommodation is provided only to students admitted to the College and to those who are from outside Delhi. Admission to the hostel is offered strictly on merit-cum-interview basis, subject to the guidelines framed by the College Hostel Committee and the University. The Bliss Hostel is quite distinct in many respects. It has a well-furnished common room, a gymnasium and subscribes to a large number of national dailies and magazines. The hostel has a cooperative mess that is managed by the hostel residents themselves. The Prospectus as well as Application Forms for admission to the Hostel will be available separately from the Hostel Office. In case of any difficulty you may please contact the Hostel Warden .You can Register as a new user or log in to your account .For any inquiries, feel free to contact us.

OUR LOCATION

Welcome to our vibrant hostel nestled in the heart of Delhi! Situated in a lively area, our hostel enjoys convenient access to metro, bus, and cab services, ensuring you have seamless transportation options to explore the city. Your stay here promises not just comfort within our walls but also the excitement and energy of the bustling locality that surrounds us.
